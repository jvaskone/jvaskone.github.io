About me

Hello! My name is Judit and I have nine years of experience in software development.
I have an MSc degree in Computer Science (University of Debrecen, Hungary).

During my career I worked as a software developer for more than seven years in the cheminformatical and medical fields.
My favourite projects were visualizing large datasets (volumetric data). It thrills me how much more we can understand data in a properly visualized way comparing to the raw format.

I also performed in the Scrum Master role for two years. In this role I have experienced how visual representations of information can transform raw data into actionable insights, empowering teams to improve their efficiency, productivity and collaboration.

These experiences have sparked my enthusiasm for data analysis and visualization and I'm excited to continue exploring this field.
